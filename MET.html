Services

Computer Science

The duration of study at the institute is four academic years. A bachelor's degree in computer science is awarded equivalent to a bachelor's degree in computers and information (computer science) granted by Egyptian universities subject to the Universities Organization Law No. 49 of 1972 and its executive regulations. The academic degree is approved by the Minister of Higher Education and the Supreme Council of Universities.

Management information systems

The duration of study at the institute is four academic years. A bachelor’s degree in management information systems equivalent to a bachelor’s degree in commerce (business administration “management information systems”) is awarded by Egyptian universities subject to the Universities Organization Law No. 49 of 1972 and its executive regulations, and the scientific degree is approved by the Minister of Higher Education and the Supreme Council of Universities.

Accounting and business administration

The duration of study at the institute is four academic years. A bachelor’s degree in accounting or business administration equivalent to a bachelor’s degree in commerce (accounting or business administration) is granted by Egyptian universities that are subject to the Universities Organization Law No. 49 of 1972 and its executive regulations. The scientific degree is approved by the Minister of Higher Education and the Supreme Council of Universities.

Vision

The Misr Higher Institute of Commerce and Computers in Mansoura aspires to be a leading academic edifice locally and regionally with the best quality standards.

message

The Misr Higher Institute of Commerce and Computers in Mansoura seeks to prepare qualified human cadres scientifically, practically and professionally in the fields of computer science, business systems, accounting and business administration to be able to make changes that keep pace with scientific and technological development by providing a stimulating learning environment within the framework of a flexible system that allows continuous development and preserves the constants educational and ethical.
